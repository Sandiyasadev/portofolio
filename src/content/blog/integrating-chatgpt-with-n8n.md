# Integrating ChatGPT with n8n: 5 Practical Use Cases

AI and automation are a powerful combination. Here are five ways to supercharge your n8n workflows with ChatGPT.

## 1. Automated Email Responses

Use ChatGPT to draft personalized email responses:
- Trigger on incoming support emails
- Analyze the email content
- Generate a context-aware response
- Send for human review or auto-send for common queries

**Time saved**: 5-10 minutes per email

## 2. Lead Qualification & Scoring

Let AI analyze lead data and provide qualification scores:
- Pull lead info from your CRM
- Send enriched data to ChatGPT
- Get a score (1-100) with reasoning
- Route to appropriate sales rep

**Result**: 30x faster lead response

## 3. Content Summarization

Process long documents into actionable summaries:
- Receive documents via webhook
- Extract text content
- Generate executive summary
- Post to Slack or save to Notion

**Use case**: Meeting notes, research papers, competitor analysis

## 4. Data Extraction & Structuring

Convert unstructured data into clean JSON:
- Receive messy text (emails, PDFs, forms)
- Ask ChatGPT to extract specific fields
- Validate the structured output
- Insert into database/CRM

**Accuracy**: 95%+ with good prompts

## 5. Intelligent Routing & Categorization

Automatically categorize and route incoming requests:
- Analyze message content
- Determine category, urgency, department
- Route to appropriate team/queue
- Tag for future analysis

**Example**: Support ticket triage

## Implementation Tips

1. **Use clear prompts**: Be specific about expected output format
2. **Include examples**: Few-shot prompting improves accuracy
3. **Validate outputs**: Always check AI responses before critical actions
4. **Handle rate limits**: Implement queuing for high-volume workflows
